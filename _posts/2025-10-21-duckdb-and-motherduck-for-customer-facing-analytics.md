Building customer‑facing analytics — for example directly in a web application — has unique performance requirements: sub‑second query response, unpredictable queries, and minimal load on production systems. Traditional OLAP approaches are often either too heavyweight or too slow for these interactive use cases. DuckDB, a lightweight analytics engine, combined with MotherDuck, which extends DuckDB into a scalable cloud platform, offers a solution that balances speed, simplicity, and tenant isolation by enabling local caching and cloud query execution.
